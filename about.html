Building a Legacy Since 1995

Presentation Higher Secondary School, Kozhikode, has a proud legacy of nurturing generations of students through strong academic values, discipline, and holistic development. Over the years, its alumni have made meaningful contributions in diverse fields.

The alumni network aims to strengthen the bond between former students and the school by providing a platform to reconnect, share experiences, and support one another. It also encourages alumni to contribute towards the growth and guidance of future students.
